Lasagna

Italian type of pasta

The lasagna is possibly the oldest type of pasta.
It is an Italian dish made of stacked layer of lasagne alternating with fillings such as ragu, vegetables, cheese, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients:
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup water
Steps:
  1. In a large pan, cook ground beef, Italian sausage and onion
  2. Stir in garlic, salt...
  3. Cover and bake
