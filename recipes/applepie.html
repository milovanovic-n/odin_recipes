Apple Pie

Apple Pie by grandma Ople

This was my grandma's recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it.

It will always be my favourite and has won several prizes in local competitions.

Ingredients:
  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-prupose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar and brown sugar
  • 8 apples - peeled, cored and sliced
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste.
    Add water, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  2. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust.
    Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.
  3. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
